About Us!

We are the best.

At SPACEGON, we are passionate about exploring the wonders of the cosmos and sharing our fascination with the universe with fellow space enthusiasts like you. Our mission is to provide a platform that ignites curiosity, fosters learning, and brings the beauty of space closer to everyone.

Our Journey!

SPACEGON, was founded by a group of astronomy enthusiasts who wanted to create a space where people could gather to celebrate the magnificence of the universe. Our journey began with a simple idea: to make astronomy and space exploration accessible to all, regardless of their level of expertise.
